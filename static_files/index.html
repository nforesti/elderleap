Welcome to A.C.T.

An Assistive Communication Tool for nonverbal children.

Image-to-Speech

image to speech

You and your child can click on easily-recognizable images, and the image will be voiced.

Image to ASL

image to asl

If your child is unable or prefers not to voice, many of the images have an American Sign Language option to help your child communicate.

Interactive Keyboard

A text-to-speech keyboard tool to help your child communicate if they can type.
