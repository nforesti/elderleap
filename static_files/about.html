About ACT

This tool is not intended for clinical use. Given the wide range of needs and abilites of nonverbal folks, we realize that our app may not be able to accomodate all of those needs. However, we hope that this tool may provide some assistance as a communication medium.

The main Dashboard is intended for children and young adults who are not inclined to type. It consists of words and images grouped together by categories. Once a category is selected, the images and phrases for that category are displayed. Clicking on a word or phrase triggers a text-to-speech vocalization. You can also combine phrases and images using the drag and drop feature.

For more advanced users, we included the Keyboard feature as a simple text-to-speech tool. Simply type in the desired phrase and click "speak" for the message to be verbalized.
